Column Explanations – AI Driven Mental Health Literacy Dataset

Demographic Information
-----------------------
Age
    Participant's age.

Gender
    Participant's gender.

Educational.Qualification
    Highest educational qualification completed.

GPT_Use
    Whether or how frequently the participant uses ChatGPT.

Pre-Intervention Measures (Before Using ChatGPT)
------------------------------------------------

pre.MHL_KMHP
    Knowledge about mental health problems.

pre.MHL_EBS
    Beliefs and misconceptions about mental health.

pre.MHL_FASHSB
    Knowledge of mental health first aid and help-seeking behavior.

pre.MHL_SHS
    Knowledge of self-help strategies for mental health.

pre.MHL_Total
    Overall mental health literacy score.

pre.MHSAS
    Confidence in seeking professional mental health support.

pre.STIG
    Level of stigma toward mental health issues.

pre.MHSES
    Confidence in managing mental health challenges.

pre.SPANE_P
    Positive emotions and experiences.

pre.SPANE_N
    Negative emotions and experiences.

pre.SPANE_B
    Overall emotional balance (positive minus negative feelings).

pre.FLOUSC
    Psychological well-being and flourishing.

Post-Intervention Measures (After Using ChatGPT)
------------------------------------------------

post.MHL_KMHP
    Knowledge about mental health problems after intervention.

post.MHL_EBS
    Beliefs and misconceptions about mental health after intervention.

post.MHL_FASHSB
    Knowledge of mental health first aid and help-seeking behavior after intervention.

post.MHL_SHS
    Knowledge of self-help strategies after intervention.

post.MHL_Total
    Overall mental health literacy score after intervention.

post.MHSAS
    Confidence in seeking professional mental health support after intervention.

post.STIG
    Level of stigma toward mental health issues after intervention.

post.MHSES
    Confidence in managing mental health challenges after intervention.

post.SPANE_P
    Positive emotions and experiences after intervention.

post.SPANE_N
    Negative emotions and experiences after intervention.

post.SPANE_B
    Overall emotional balance after intervention.

post.FLOUSC
    Psychological well-being and flourishing after intervention.

ChatGPT Evaluation
------------------

GPT_Rating
    Overall rating given to ChatGPT.

Prompts_Rating
    Rating of the prompts or interactions used.

Intervention_Rating
    Rating of the overall intervention experience.

MHL_Rating
    Rating of ChatGPT's usefulness for mental health literacy.

GPT_MHL
    Whether the participant felt ChatGPT improved their mental health literacy.

GPT_Flaw
    Whether the participant noticed any flaws or limitations in ChatGPT responses.
